    I started getting very into Russian music back in late March of this year due to this mix on YouTube. My first impression was that it sounded unexpectedly similar to Citypop– specifically the first track, which I've included in my own playlist as the 13th track (although I completely forgot about the YT mix & rediscovered it on everynoise.) I'm not sure how to describe how I feel about this music. I think 'nostalgic' isn't quite accurate. Obviously it's mostly pop/rock, but I think there's an element of it being a non-English language that creates a strange illusion of nostalgia for me.

    This playlist consists of 70s-00s Slavic music. Mostly Russian, some Ukrainian, and some Bulgarian. It is loosely sorted based on release date and genre, but mostly curated based on vibe ? I hope it flows well?

    Also, I've written notes for some of the songs, so read them if you are interested!
