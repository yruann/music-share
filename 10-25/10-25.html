☆ (10.23.2025)

Due to (semi-)popular demand, here is my 70s-2010s Slavic music playlist. Mostly Russian, some Ukrainian, and some Bulgarian. It is loosely sorted based on release date and genre, but mostly curated based on personal preference. I've written notes for some of the songs, so read them if you are interested! My favorite tracks are highlighted.

I started getting very into Russian music back in late March of this year due to this mix on YouTube. My first impression was that it sounded unexpectedly similar to Citypop– specifically the first track, which I've included in my own playlist as the 13th track (although I completely forgot about the YT mix & rediscovered it on everynoise.) I'm not sure how to describe how I feel about this music. It feels silly to say it sounds 'nostalgic', but there's some element of familiarity that makes it easy to listen to outside of the fact that it was obviously influenced by other popular music of the time.

Note: The dates for many of these songs may be incorrect or may not match the date listed on streaming platforms, as many of them were released in multiple albums, with the earlier ones often not appearing on streaming platforms. The following release dates were simply the earliest ones I could find for each track.
